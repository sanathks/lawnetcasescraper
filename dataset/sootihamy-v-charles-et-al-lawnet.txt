Restitutio in integrum—Procedure governed by chapter XXIV. of ike
Civil Procedure Code—Respondent to begin.
Where on an application for restitutio in integrum notice wasissued to the respondent, and he appears he has the right to begin.
The application for restitutio in integrum is by way of summaryprocedure, and is governed by the provisions of chapter XXIV.of the Civil Procedure Code. Upon such an application section 377prescribes the order which should be made. It should be either anorder nisi or an interlocutory order. Section 379 indicates theform of the order and the mode of service.
