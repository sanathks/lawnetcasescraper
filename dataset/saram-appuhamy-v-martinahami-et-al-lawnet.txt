Rea judicata—Judgment in partition suit—-Action rei vindicatio—Civil Procedure Code, s. 207.
Where a partition suit was dismissed on the ground that thedefendant had acquired title by prescription to the land, and wherethe defendant subsequently brought an action to vindicate histitle to the land pleading the judgment in the partition suit as resjudicata on the question of title,—
Held, the judgment in the partition suit operated as res judicata,and prevented the parties from raising the question of title again.Fernando v. Menikrcda1 referred to and distinguished.
